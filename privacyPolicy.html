Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We collect personal information such as your name, email, and profile data when you register for an account. We also collect data related to your artwork submissions and activity (votes, comments).

How We Use This Information

  • To manage your account and allow you to participate in the platform's activities (art submissions, voting, comments).
  • To improve the platform and provide a better user experience.
  • To communicate important updates and notifications regarding the platform.

Data Sharing and Security

We do not share your personal data with third parties without your consent, except when required by law. We use encryption and secure servers to protect your data from unauthorized access.

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, including remembering login details and preferences. You can opt out of cookies via your browser settings, but this may affect site functionality.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@artspace.com.

Data Retention

Your data, including artwork submissions, will be retained as long as your account remains active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. You will be notified of any significant changes via email or the platform.
