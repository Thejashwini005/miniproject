Support

Contact Information

If you are experiencing issues or need help, feel free to reach out to us via email at support@artspace.com.

FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

  • How do I submit my artwork? - Go to the "Submit Art" section and follow the upload instructions.
  • How can I edit or remove my artwork? - You can manage your submissions in your profile under the "My Art" section.
  • How does voting work? - Each user can vote on their favorite artwork. Votes help us determine which pieces will be featured in exhibitions.
  • What should I do if I find inappropriate content? - Please report the artwork or comment to our team, and we will investigate immediately.

Technical Support

If you are facing technical issues, try refreshing the page or clearing your browser's cache. For further assistance, contact our technical support team.

Guidance for Artists

For optimal submissions, ensure your artwork is high-resolution (minimum 1024px) and in a commonly supported format (JPEG, PNG). Use proper titles and descriptions to enhance viewer engagement.

Exhibition Help

If you wish to have your artwork featured in a special exhibition, you can apply through the "Exhibitions" tab. Our team will review and get back to you shortly.
